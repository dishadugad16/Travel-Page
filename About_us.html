Travel is the movement of people between distant geographical locations. Travel can be done by foot, bicycle, automobile, train, boat, bus, airplane, ship or other means, with or without luggage, and can be one way or round trip. Travel can also include relatively short stays between successive movements, as in the case of tourism.

Mia's Travel Page is all about cultivating positive vibes about travel. It aims to entertain the general public and showcase the beautiful scenography and photography. We have also been featured in the Traveller's magazine. Our mission is to be the leading and most trustworthy travel page that sets the standard for professionalism, reliability and transparency to its customers.
